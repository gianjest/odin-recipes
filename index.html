Lasagna

Lasagna alla Bolognese is an institution, the baked lasagna par excellence, the signature Sunday dish. This rich and flavorful dish originated in Emilia and, specifically, in the city of Bologna. Baked lasagna, however, is known, appreciated, made and remade for the holidays and beyond, tasted and shared throughout Italy and abroad just as an iconic Italian dish.




Linguine gamberetti zucchine e zafferano

Zucchini and shrimp? An unbeatable duo, which we wanted to bring out the best in this recipe: linguine shrimp, zucchini and saffron. A variation of pasta and zucchini with a creamy and velvety sauce, easy to make even without adding cream thanks to a tasty bisque!




Risotto alla milanese

Risotto alla Milanese needs no introduction. Very few ingredients for a historic dish that has delighted thousands of palates, Italian and otherwise. It is one of Milan's representative recipes, with its golden color given by precious saffron pistils and irresistible creaminess. A delicious, unique dish with which you can win over your guests.
